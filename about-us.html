About us

SHREE RAJ INDUSTRIES has been a reputable producer of Geared Elevator Traction Machines since 2017. We manufacture traction

We manufacture traction machines with the Brand Name “SHREE RAJ” under the leadership of Mr. Basant Lal Yadav, founder, and proprietor of Shree Raj Industries with more than 20 years of expertise in the elevator business; we aim to become India’s leading manufacturer of geared elevator traction machines.


Our Elevator Traction Machines are available with payload capacity of408 Kg to 3500 kg and travel speeds ranging from 0.30 m/sec-1m/sec. We use the highest quality raw materials while manufacturing our machines, ensuring that each one is of the highest caliber. Ensure the materials are machined with appropriate, precise, and accurate measurement tools.


The confidence and contentment of our valuable customers, which inspire us to improve tomorrow and make a contribution to the vertical transportation business, are the company’s most precious assets.


SHREE RAJ INDUSTRIES is committed to giving customers the best after-sales service. The final happiness of our esteemed clientele validates the business’s dedication to the best practices and design supported by value business ethics.
